About the Experiment

$(".dragMe").draggable({

The experiment is about "draggable" feature of jQueryUI. The components marked as draggable can be moved around. In the experiment, the first paragraph and blue part of second paragraph are draggable. The above code snippet shows hw this can be achieved. The paragraphs that are draggable() method, were bound under the id "dragMe".
